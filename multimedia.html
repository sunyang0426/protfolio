TV Newspackage: Solidarity with Paris

21 Peaceworks members gathered in front of the city hall of Columbia, Missouri in solidarity with those meeting in Paris Climate Conference. They called for climate awareness from local communities and more drastic actions from international community.

TV Newspackage: Girls on the Run

Girls on the run 5K is a non-profit program in public schools throughout US and Canada. It aims to educate girls at 3rd to 5th grade self-esteem and deal with peer pressure by weekly 5K training.
