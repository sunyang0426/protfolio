Hello, my name is Yang, and I am currently a graduate student at University of Missouri, pursuing my master degree on journalism. My sequences are convergence reporting (multimedia) and data journalism.

Before came to United States, I was a political journalism in China. I enjoyed my job covering Beijing, and the elite group of China. Nonetheless, I realized digging into data to find out the naked truth, and recording the stories of normal people are my real affection.

I have been frequentluy travelling between China, United States and Europe since these years. I would like to apply my knowledge and my experience to further understand the most trio-lateral relation in today's international community.
